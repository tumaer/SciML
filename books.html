Books

Books#

This is a list of recommended books to accompany the lecture

  1. Probabilistic Machine Learning: An Introduction, by Kevin P Murphy

  2. Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning, by Christopher M Bishop

  3. Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning, by Carl E Rasmusses, and Christopher KI Williams

  4. Data-Driven Science and Engineering: Machine Learning, Dynamical Systems, and Control by Steven L Brunton, and J Nathan Kutz

    • Available through the university library as an e-book.

  5. An Introduction to Statistical Learning, by Gareth James, Daniela Witten, Trevor Hastie, and Robert Tibshirani

  6. Patterns, Predictions, and Actions: A Story about Machine Learning, by Moritz Hardt and Benjamin Recht

  7. Dive into Deep Learning, by Aston Zhang, Zachary C Lipton, Mu Li, and Alexander J Smola

  8. Understanding Computational Bayesian Statistics, by William M Bolstad
